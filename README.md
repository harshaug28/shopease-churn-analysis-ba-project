## 🔍 Key Findings

- **Top churn driver:** Poor post-purchase support experience (38% of churned customers cited this)
- **High-risk segment:** Customers with 0 purchases in last 45 days + 2+ support tickets
- **Revenue impact:** Retaining just 5% more customers = ~$780K additional annual revenue
- **Quick win identified:** Personalized re-engagement email at day 30 of inactivity reduced churn by 12% in pilot
